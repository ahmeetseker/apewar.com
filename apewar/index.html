About Game

  • About

APEWAR play to earn is a fight and actionn game.Players can play buy the NFT character or they can play with given free (very low power)NFT character. Players who spawn in random areas on the map earn points by killing each other. Whoever gets more points at the end of the game wins the game. Rewards are distributed based on player numbers and your place in the ranking.
